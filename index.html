Support a bold dream—build an original Indian animation studio that tells our stories with global-quality visuals and heart.

Hi, I’m Gowtham.

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve loved animation. Like many of you, I grew up watching cartoons, anime, and animated movies—but most of them were made outside India. That always made me wonder: why aren’t Indian stories told through animation at a global level?

India has amazing stories. We have culture, mythology, history, and raw creative talent. But what we don’t have is enough support for Indian animators to tell our own stories—our way. That’s why I’m starting something big.

Why I’m Raising These Funds

This fundraiser is to set up the first phase of the studio—finding a space, getting basic equipment, and building a small but talented team. I want to start production on our first short film by the end of this year (2025).

Where Your Money Goes

  • Studio space and equipment (computers, software, drawing tablets)
  • Hiring young Indian animators and artists
  • Producing our first short film
  • Creating opportunities for fresh talent through workshops and mentorship

What This Means

This isn’t just about one studio. It’s about building a movement. A place where Indian stories are told beautifully, powerfully, and with the kind of quality we’re used to seeing from big international studios.

I can’t do this alone. Your support will help turn this dream into reality.

Even a small contribution makes a big difference. If you believe in Indian stories, creativity, and the power of animation—please support and share.
